Recipe Of Chicken Pakodi

Ingredients Needed

  • Chicken
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Corn flour
  • Flour
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Green chillies

  • Spices required

    1. Salt
    2. Chilli powder
    3. Turmeric
    4. Garam masala
    5. Chicken masala

Procedure

  1. Wash chicken properly using turmeric.
  2. Mix all spices into a bowl along with ginger garlic paste and add it to chicken.
  3. Leave it for sometime(about 2 hours) to get marinated.
  4. Heat oil in a pan.
  5. Until the oil is heated:mix chicken with corn flour,flour and green chillies.
  6. Then slowly add small pieces of chicken into the oil on a medium flame.
